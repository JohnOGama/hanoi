What is Hanoi best known for?

What is Hanoi famous for? With many cultural, historical, and architectural landmarks, Hanoi offers visitors a rich tapestry of experiences. Some notable landmarks in the city include Hoan Kiem Lake, a serene oasis steeped in legend, and Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, which stands in Ba Dinh Square as a revered symbol.
